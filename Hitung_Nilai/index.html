Formula Nilai Gabungan 2026

NG = (Total Rapor × 0.4) + ((TKA + TKAD) × 0.6)
Rapor: Rata-rata 4 mapel × 5 semester → jumlah (maks 400)
Ujian: TKA (Mat + B.Indo) + TKAD (B.Inggris + IPA) → jumlah (maks 400)
